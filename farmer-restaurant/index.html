How The Cycle Works

A mutually beneficial relationship that regenerates the earth while elevating culinary experiences.

↑

Soil Regeneration

The organic waste is returned to the farms and composted, enriching the soil for the next generation of crops. The cycle begins anew.

About our journey...

EcoCycle was founded with a singular mission: to eliminate food waste while elevating the culinary ecosystem. We realized that local farms were struggling with the high costs of organic fertilizers, while premium restaurants were throwing away nutrient-dense food scraps.

By connecting the two, we've created a closed-loop system that solves problems for both partners. Farms supply restaurants with hyper-local, peak-fresh ingredients. Restaurants return their organic trimmings, which we compost and return to the earth to grow the next harvest.
